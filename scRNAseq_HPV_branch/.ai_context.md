# Classify cells by viral expression
seurat_obj$hpv_status <- ifelse(

## Gotchas & Warnings

1. **Viral gene detection**: May miss low-abundance viral transcripts
2. **Integration sites**: Require WGS data - not detectable from RNA alone
3. **HPV types**: Specify HPV16 vs HPV18 - different oncogenes
4. **Normal cells**: Stromal cells should be HPV negative (internal control)

## Common Errors & Solutions

### Error: "Insufficient cells"
**Cause**: Rare cell types have too few cells  
**Solution**: Merge similar cell types or collect more data

### Error: "Memory allocation failed"
**Cause**: Large datasets  
**Solution**: Use sketch-based methods or increase RAM
