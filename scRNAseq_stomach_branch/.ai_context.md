## Gotchas & Warnings

1. **Anatomical regions**: Corpus, antrum, fundus have different cells
2. **Disease context**: Gastric atrophy, H. pylori infection alter composition
3. **Pit vs gland**: Different depths have different cell types
4. **Antral G-cells**: Rare population - may be missed in small datasets

## Common Errors & Solutions

### Error: "Insufficient cells"
**Cause**: Rare cell types have too few cells  
**Solution**: Merge similar cell types or collect more data

### Error: "Memory allocation failed"
**Cause**: Large datasets  
**Solution**: Use sketch-based methods or increase RAM
