## Gotchas & Warnings

1. **Radius matters**: Too small = sparse, too large = washed out
2. **Cell type resolution**: Use broad categories for robust neighborhoods
3. **Spatial FOV**: Edge spots have fewer neighbors
4. **Statistics**: Use permutation tests to assess significance

## Common Errors & Solutions

### Error: "Coordinate mismatch"
**Cause**: Image and data coordinates don't align  
**Solution**: Check `scale.factors` in spatial object

### Error: "Memory error"
**Cause**: High-resolution images  
**Solution**: Downsample images or use cropped regions
