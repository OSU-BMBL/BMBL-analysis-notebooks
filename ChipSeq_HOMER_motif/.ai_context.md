## Gotchas & Warnings

1. **Background selection**: Critical for meaningful results - use matched background
2. **Peak number**: Need 100+ peaks for robust motif finding
3. **Sequence context**: HOMER uses 200bp around peak centers
4. **Genome version**: Must specify correct genome (hg38, mm10, etc.)
