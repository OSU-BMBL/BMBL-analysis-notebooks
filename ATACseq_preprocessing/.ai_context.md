## Gotchas & Warnings

1. **Tn5 adapter trimming**: Essential for ATAC - use correct adapters
2. **Mitochondrial reads**: High mito% indicates poor quality
3. **Peak calling**: Use --shift and --extsize for ATAC (-100, 200)
4. **Replicates**: Call peaks on pooled replicates, not individually
