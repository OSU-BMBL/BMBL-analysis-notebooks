## Gotchas & Warnings

1. **File size**: Large datasets (>50k cells) make slow apps
2. **Server required**: For sharing, need shinyapps.io or RStudio Connect
3. **Gene limits**: Too many genes slow down - select HVGs only
4. **Updates**: Re-run entire workflow to update with new data

## Common Errors & Solutions

### Error: "Insufficient cells"
**Cause**: Rare cell types have too few cells  
**Solution**: Merge similar cell types or collect more data

### Error: "Memory allocation failed"
**Cause**: Large datasets  
**Solution**: Use sketch-based methods or increase RAM
