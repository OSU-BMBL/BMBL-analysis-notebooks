## Gotchas & Warnings

1. **Bisulfite conversion**: Check conversion rate (>99% needed)
2. **Genome preparation**: Bismark needs bisulfite-converted genome
3. **Coverage depth**: 10x minimum per CpG for reliable calls
4. **SNPs**: Can confound methylation calls - filter known SNPs
