## Gotchas & Warnings

1. **Input control**: Essential for accurate peak calling
2. **Antibody quality**: Check enrichment at known sites
3. **Peak calling**: Narrow for TFs, broad for histones
4. **Reproducibility**: Use IDR for reproducible peaks across replicates
