## Quick Summary

Low-pass whole genome sequencing for copy number and karyotyping.

**Typical Runtime**: 4-6 hours  
**Memory Required**: 16GB RAM  
**Input**: Low-coverage WGS BAM files (0.1-1x)  
**Output**: Copy number profiles, ploidy, large-scale rearrangements

## Gotchas & Warnings

1. **Coverage requirements**: 0.1x minimum, 0.5x recommended
2. **Bin size**: Larger bins (1Mb) for noisy low-pass data
3. **Normal control**: Essential for accurate CN calling
4. **Resolution**: Detects large CN changes (>10Mb), not focal
