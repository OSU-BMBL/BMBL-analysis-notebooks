## Gotchas & Warnings

1. **Python required**: CellOracle is Python-based (uses reticulate)
2. **Base GRN**: Needs scATAC or prior knowledge of enhancers
3. **Memory intensive**: Large networks require substantial RAM
4. **Computation**: Very slow for >10k cells - consider downsampling
